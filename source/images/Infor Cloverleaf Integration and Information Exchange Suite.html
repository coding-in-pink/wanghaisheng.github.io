Infor Cloverleaf Integration and Information Exchange Suite

The secure exchange of data and health information meets your meaningful use requirements and, more importantly, lays the foundation for emerging reimbursement models and accountable care. Infor Cloverleaf Integration and Information Exchange Suite help you streamline the exchange of healthcare data within your organization, with partners, and across your community of care. These leading clinical integration technologies create a sustainable foundation for you to proactively manage, coordinate, and share data across your care continuum to improve quality and care outcomes.

ION, Cloverleaf, and Infor Mingle™ Interaction for Healthcare

Cloverleaf manages patient information workflow.

Watch the demo 

Infor Cloverleaf solutions help you with:

  • Connecting data—Implement high-performance, high capacity integration to meet your interoperability requirements and improve data flow.  You get secure integration, messaging, monitoring, archiving, resiliency, and cost efficient interoperability via industry standard protocols and web services, regardless of the source system, message format, or transmission protocol.   
  • Connecting doctors—Empower physicians, inside and outside your four walls, with the information they need to operate, while eliminating the frustrations of time-consuming, labor-intensive, back-and-forth paper pushing.
  • Connecting communities—Integrate clinical data from across disparate entities and technologies to facilitate access to and retrieval of clinical data in a safe, secure, timely, and equitable manner.

The Infor Cloverleaf difference

Infor Cloverleaf is a recognized world leader in healthcare integration and information exchange across disparate clinical, financial, and operational information systems. Cloverleaf was developed exclusively for the healthcare industry to facilitate high-performance, high-capacity integration and information exchange. These solutions were designed to meet interoperability requirements and enable secure data flow within and between healthcare providers. For over 20 years, Cloverleaf has been the standard for healthcare integration.

Infor Cloverleaf Integration Suite

A market-leading, enterprise-caliber integration platform that facilitates the movement of secure data through disparate systems within and outside the healthcare enterprise.

Learn more

Infor Cloverleaf Cloud Health Information Exchange

A secure, standards-based hosted HIE infrastructure that allows providers, payers, and government entities to share data and collaborate on care. This cloud-based service reduces the need for large capital expenditures, allows for rapid deployment, and facilitates sustainability for its users.

Learn more

IBM Enterprise Master Person Index (EMPI)

A flexible tool that helps resolve patient and provider identities across disparate systems. It provides the highest level of patient matching accuracy to eliminate duplicate patient records that often plague the creation of an enterprise patient record, or electronic health record (EHR).

Learn more

Infor Lawson Clinical Bridge

Best-practices, pre-built connections between Infor Lawson business applications and key clinical systems. Powered by the Infor Cloverleaf Integration Engine, the Infor Lawson Clinical Bridge links clinical, financial, and operational systems.
